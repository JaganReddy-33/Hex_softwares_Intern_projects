Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives that cover rescue, medical care, rehabilitation, community outreach, and rehoming.

24/7 Rescue Response

Rapid-response teams equipped to rescue injured or distressed animals from streets, accidents, and abusive situations.

What we do: Emergency extraction, triage on-site, transport to clinic, temporary shelter setup.

Medical & Surgical Care

On-site vets and partner clinics handle surgeries, vaccinations, microchipping, and long-term treatments.

What we do: Spay/neuter, wound care, X-rays, reconstructive surgeries, physiotherapy.

Rehabilitation & Behavior

Rehab for trauma, socialization classes, behavior modification to help animals adjust for adoption.

What we do: Feeding programs, mobility aids, socialization, enrichment activities.

Adoption & Fostering

We match animals with vetted adoptive families and coordinate foster-care for long recoveries.

What we do: Home checks, adoption counseling, trial periods and post-adoption follow-up.

Community Outreach

Education programs in schools & neighborhoods to promote humane treatment and responsible pet care.

What we do: Sterilization drives, feeding camps, awareness workshops.

Wildlife Assistance

Rescue service for urban wildlife and coordination with wildlife rehab centers for species-specific needs.
